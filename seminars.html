Seminars

National Youth Leadership Forum: Engineering

A week long engineering program that taught us critical thinking, creative thinking, electrical engineering, some programming, and the 17 sustainable development goals.
We worked in teams of 3-5 to get our projects done. The main task throughout the week was to come up with an invention that related to 3 or more sustainable development goals and to work as a team to come up with the function, design, budget, materials list, and other details.
